Treatment Dataset — Pan-Drug CNN+Transformer Classifier with Drug Conditioning
Dataset: 79,751 rows, 9 drugs, full cDNA sequences from Ensembl REST API
Context: ±45 nt around stop codon (93 nt window), extracted at training time
Architecture: nt embedding (32) + drug embedding (32, injected at every position)
             → 2x Conv1D (64ch, k=5/3, GELU, LN, dropout=0.3)
             → 2x pre-norm Transformer (d=64, h=4, ffn=128, local attn band=7)
             → drug-conditioned cross-attn query pool → MLP (64→32→1)
Training: BCEWithLogitsLoss, per-sample weights from drug-specific class imbalance
          WeightedRandomSampler, AdamW lr=5e-4, wd=1e-3, batch=1024, max_epochs=100
          Early stopping on val AUPRC (patience=20), ReduceLROnPlateau
CV: 5-fold StratifiedKFold (stratified on drug×label), per-drug threshold tuning
GPU: H100 80GB HBM3
